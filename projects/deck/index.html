The Problem

Agents run their day across 6+ apps

Calendar, MLS, CRM, email, GPS, and spreadsheets. Context switching kills productivity. No single view of "what matters today."

  • Missed follow-ups — leads go cold because the reminder was in a different app
  • No route intelligence — agents drive 50+ miles/day with no optimized path
  • Market blindness — rate changes and inventory shifts buried in weekly emails
  • Meeting prep — scrambling to remember client details in the car
Our Approach

One screen. Everything for today.

We explored 7 different approaches to find the right daily command center — from timeline views to map-first routing to deal dashboards.

Concept A — Property-Led Day

The next showing is the homepage

Full-bleed property hero image. The page feels like a listing — but it's your schedule. Scroll down for pipeline, leads, and commission.

  • Hero = next property — instant visual context
  • Schedule as event cards — horizontal scroll with property thumbnails
  • Commission pipeline — see your money flowing
Concept B — Deal Dashboard

Cockpit-style with live filters

No hero. Maximum data density. Filter deals by type, toggle Today/This Week. Real photos, meeting context on every card.

  • Dynamic deal cards — filter by touring, showing, listing, pending, closing
  • Market pulse — what changed this week + steady-state snapshot
  • Meeting context — who you're meeting and why on every card
Concept C — Day Planner

Calendar cards for the week ahead

Day picker strip at the top — tap any day to see that day's deals, schedule, and stats. Week-at-a-glance shows the full picture.

  • Day picker — colored dots show appointment types per day
  • Everything updates — cards, schedule, stats, CTA all respond
  • Week summary — see appointment density across the week
Concept D — Route Day

Map-first with swipeable stops

Interactive map with 4 color-coded pins. Swipe between event cards — the map pans and pins highlight in sync. Drive time itinerary below.

  • Real map — Leaflet + OpenStreetMap, no API key needed
  • Card ↔ Map sync — swipe cards, map follows
  • Route intelligence — drive times, total mileage, optimal order

Recommendation

Combine the best of each

The final product doesn't have to pick one. We recommend a modular approach — the best elements from each concept composed into one experience.

  • Day picker from v5b — agents plan ahead, not just today
  • Deal cards with context from v5 — who, why, and meeting prep on every card
  • Route map from v6 — embedded above the schedule for drive-day awareness
  • Market pulse from v5 — what changed + snapshot, updated daily
  • Commission pipeline from v5 — agents are motivated by money flow visibility
Next Steps

From prototype to product

  • User testing — put all 4 concepts in front of 5 agents, measure task completion and preference
  • Data integration plan — map to MLS, CRM, and calendar APIs for real data
  • Build the composite — merge winning elements into one Remix + Polaris app
  • Beta program — 10 agents, 30 days, measure daily active usage
